28    * Implements the Ethiopic calendar system, which defines every fourth year as
29    * leap, much like the Julian calendar. The year is broken down into 12 months,
30    * each 30 days in length. An extra period at the end of the year is either 5
31    * or 6 days in length. In this implementation, it is considered a 13th month.
32    * <p>
33    * Year 1 in the Ethiopic calendar began on August 29, 8 CE (Julian), thus

36    * <p>
